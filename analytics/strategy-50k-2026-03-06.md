## Part 9 — What NOT to Do

1. **Don't chase ClearTax on their core keyword ("income tax filing")** — they have 9M visitors and thousands of backlinks. Pick gaps they ignore.
2. **Don't add login/registration** — frictionless is the product. Every login prompt cuts conversion by 50-80%.
3. **Don't add paid features before 30k/month traffic** — not worth the complexity. Ads and affiliate first.
4. **Don't spread thin on social media** — at this stage, SEO compounds. Twitter/Instagram don't. One LinkedIn post per major article = enough.
5. **Don't ignore filing season** — it's the 3x traffic window. If you're not ready by June 1, you miss the wave until 2027.
