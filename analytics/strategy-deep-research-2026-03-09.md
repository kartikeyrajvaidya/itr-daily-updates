## The 3 Clusters That Win

### Cluster 1: India Income Data (Unique Moat — Build This First)
Only ITR Stats has live CBDT data. No competitor covers this consumer-facing.

Pages to build:
- "Is ₹X lakh salary good in India?" — one anchor page + 6 salary variants (5L, 8L, 10L, 12L, 15L, 20L)
- "Top 1% income in India — CBDT data decoded"
- "India income distribution 2024 — what the numbers say"
- "State-wise income thresholds — Bihar to Maharashtra"
- "ITR processing statistics — live tracker"

**Why it works:** Quora threads and small blogs currently dominate. A data-rich, chart-backed ITR Stats page with CBDT citations and an embedded calculator would rank #1-3 within 3 months. The income percentile calculator already exists — these pages are the SEO wrapper around it.

### Cluster 2: ITR Season Content (Time-Sensitive — Build Before June 1)
These keywords spike 5–10x in July. Plant content now so it's indexed and ranking.

Pages to build:
- "Documents required for ITR filing 2026" (interactive checklist)
- "ITR 1 vs ITR 2 vs ITR 3 vs ITR 4 — decision tool"
- "AIS vs 26AS — what they are and what to do when they don't match"
- "How long does ITR refund take? Live data 2026"
- "ITR filing deadline 2026 — will it be extended?" (publish June, update as news breaks)

**Why it works:** These are high-volume, low-to-medium competition queries. ClearTax ranks for them but with text-only content from 2022-23. ITR Stats can win with fresh data, dark mode, and visual tables.

### Cluster 3: Calculator Gap-Fills (Build April–May)
Specific calculators competitors haven't updated or don't do well:

- **Capital Gains Calculator (Budget 2024 compliant)** — most existing tools don't handle the July 23, 2024 LTCG rate change. ITR Stats' version with a pre/post comparison would be unique.
- **Advance Tax Calculator + Due Date Calendar** — no one combines the liability calculation with the installment schedule + late payment interest (Section 234B/234C).
- **80C Optimizer** — not just a calculator but a recommender: given your income + EPF + risk profile, what's the best 80C allocation?
- **"Which ITR form?" Decision Tree** — 5-question flowchart. No competitor has a standalone public tool.

## Top 10 Content Gaps (Specific Articles That Don't Exist Well)

1. **"Is ₹10 lakh salary good in India?"** — Quora/Reddit currently ranks. No authoritative data page using CBDT percentile data exists.
2. **"AIS vs 26AS vs TIS — visual comparison 2025-26"** — All existing articles are text-heavy walls. A visual table + flowchart for "what to do when they don't match" would win.
3. **"Capital gains tax India — everything changed on July 23, 2024"** — Most calculators are outdated. A clear pre/post comparison article + tool.
4. **"India income distribution — what CBDT data actually shows"** — Academic/NGO sites have data; no consumer-friendly, chart-rich version exists.
5. **"ITR 1 vs ITR 2 — interactive decision tool"** — ClearTax has a text article. ITR Stats can build the actual tool.
6. **"New tax regime 2025 — who actually benefits? CBDT data analysis"** — Everyone has a generic article. No one has layered the CBDT income distribution on top of the break-even calculation to show *how many* taxpayers benefit.
7. **"ITR processing speed 2025-26 — live statistics"** — ITR Stats is the *only* site tracking this. Turn the dashboard into a long-form article with embedded charts.
8. **"How to check if your employer deducted TDS correctly"** — Practical step-by-step with Form 26AS integration. Currently scattered across ClearTax/TaxBuddy but no clean guide exists.
9. **"Advance tax India — who must pay, how to calculate, and what happens if you miss"** — Most articles are outdated (pre-2025 rates). Combine with a live calculator.
10. **"₹12 lakh zero tax under new regime — what it actually means (with calculator)"** — Budget 2025 created massive confusion. The rebate is ₹60,000 and only applies to income up to ₹12L. Most articles miss the nuance. A clear explainer with example calculations.

## Top 5 Feature Gaps (No Competitor Has These)

**1. Shareable income percentile card**
After the calculator shows your result, generate a shareable card: "I'm in the top X% of 8.5 crore Indian taxpayers — itrstats.in". US equivalent went viral on Twitter. Indian market equivalent doesn't exist. Add canvas-based card generation to the existing calculator. Low dev effort, high viral potential.

**2. ITR refund status email alert**
Enter your acknowledgement number + email → get pinged when status changes. No private site in India does this. Official portal sends one email, nothing more. This creates repeat visits, signup list, and genuine utility.

**4. 80C optimizer**
Input: income, existing EPF, risk tolerance, lock-in preference → Output: recommended allocation across PPF, ELSS, NPS, insurance with tradeoff chart. Tax2win has a checklist. Nobody has a true optimizer.

**5. Live ITR filing season dashboard with "will we beat last year?" tracker**
During July 2026, daily ITR filing count + "X% to last year's record" progress bar + shareable tweet cards. This is ITR Stats' biggest unique asset — a real-time data story no competitor can fake.

## The 5 Things That Will Make or Break 50K

1. **The income cluster must start getting clicks before June.** If "is 10 lakh salary good" + "top 1% income" articles rank and drive traffic through April-May, they build the domain authority that makes the filing-season content rank in July.

2. **Shareable income percentile card must ship in March.** One viral tweet about income percentile could drive 5,000–20,000 visits in a week. This has precedent in the Indian market (Ankur Warikoo, Nikhil Kamath have driven huge traffic to income data content).

3. **Capital gains calculator must be live before April 1.** March 31 = financial year end. Post-March 31 searches for "how are my capital gains taxed?" spike immediately. Miss this window and wait until next year.

4. **All filing-season content must be indexed before June 1.** Google takes 2–4 weeks to index and rank new content. Content published in June for July deadline queries will arrive too late to rank.

5. **Fix the india-income-percentile page CTR (P0 from GSC report).** It's getting 2,931 impressions/28 days with 0 clicks. Fixing the title + meta + adding Bihar/UP data could unlock 100+ clicks/week with zero new content. Do this before anything else.
