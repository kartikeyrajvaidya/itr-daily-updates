When Will Everyone Get Their Refund?

Let's do some quick math:

If the IT Department maintains this pace (~2L/day average):

37.8 lakh √∑ 2 lakh/day = ~19 days to clear the entire backlog

That's late February / early March 2026!

But here's the thing - they're actually accelerating. Feb 13 alone saw 5.69 lakh processed. If they maintain even half that pace, the backlog could clear in under 2 weeks!

The Feb 13 Anomaly

Feb 13 is interesting - it shows 5.69 lakh returns processed, but 0 new verifications. This could mean:

  • The IT portal data had a partial update (only processing data refreshed)
  • A massive batch processing run happened
  • End-of-week catch-up processing

Either way, the processing numbers are real - and they're spectacular. The backlog has dropped by nearly 14 lakh in just one week!

What To Do If You're Still Waiting

  1. Check your e-verification status - Your return won't be processed until it's verified
  2. Make sure your bank account is pre-validated - This can cause delays at the last step
  3. Check for notices - Login to the e-filing portal and see if there are any pending actions
  4. Be patient (but hopeful) - The numbers are moving in the right direction

Pro tip: If it's been more than 60 days since you e-verified and still no refund, raise a grievance on the e-Nivaran portal. But given the current pace, you might want to wait another week or two first.
