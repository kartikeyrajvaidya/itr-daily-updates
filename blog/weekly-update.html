ITR Processing Weekly Update

Last updated: Feb 10, 2026 (Week of Feb 3 - Feb 10)

February 10, 2026 4 min read

TL;DR

  • IT Department went beast mode - processing jumped from 25K/day to 2.3 lakh/day
  • 4.28 lakh returns cleared in just 2 days (Feb 8-9)
  • Pending backlog down to 47.2 lakh
  • At this rate, backlog could clear by early March

When Will Everyone Get Their Refund?

Let's do some quick math:

If the IT Department maintains this pace (2L/day):

47.2 lakh ÷ 2 lakh/day = ~24 days to clear the entire backlog

That's early March 2026!

Of course, they might not keep up this speed. But even if they do half of this, we're looking at the backlog clearing much sooner than anyone expected.

The Curious Case of Feb 4-5

You might have noticed Feb 4 and 5 were unusually slow (just 1,203 and 3,698 processed). We're not sure why - could be:

  • System maintenance
  • Weekend/holiday effect
  • Batch processing (saving up for the big push?)

Whatever the reason, the slowdown was followed by the biggest processing days we've ever recorded. Make of that what you will.

What To Do If You're Still Waiting

  1. Check your e-verification status - Your return won't be processed until it's verified
  2. Make sure your bank account is pre-validated - This can cause delays at the last step
  3. Check for notices - Login to the e-filing portal and see if there are any pending actions
  4. Be patient (but hopeful) - The numbers are moving in the right direction

Pro tip: If it's been more than 60 days since you e-verified and still no refund, raise a grievance on the e-Nivaran portal. But given the current pace, you might want to wait another week or two first.
