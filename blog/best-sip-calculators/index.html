7 Best SIP Calculators Online for 2026

We tested every popular SIP calculator in India for accuracy, step-up SIP support, and chart quality. Here's which ones actually use the right formula.

February 26, 2026 9 min read Tool Comparison

SIP calculators are one of the most Googled financial tools in India — and for good reason. Whether you're starting a ₹500 SIP or investing ₹50,000 a month, knowing what your corpus will look like in 10, 20, or 30 years matters.

But here's the problem: not all SIP calculators are built the same. Some use the wrong formula (dividing the annual rate by 12 instead of proper compounding), some don't support step-up SIP, and most give you a single number with no visual context.

We tested 7 of the most popular SIP calculators in India with identical inputs — ₹10,000/month, 12% annual return, 20 years — and compared their results, features, and accuracy. The correct answer is ₹99,91,479.

Quick Verdict

  • Best overall: ITR Stats — real-time results, step-up SIP, interactive doughnut + bar charts, year-by-year breakdown table, dark mode
  • Best for mutual fund investors: Groww — clean UI, links directly to fund investment
  • Best for tax planning: ClearTax — SIP calculator integrated with ELSS tax-saving flow
  • Best for goal planning: ET Money — slider UI with goal-based recommendations

What We Tested

For each calculator, we checked:

  • Formula accuracy — uses correct monthly compounding: i = (1 + annual_rate)^(1/12) - 1
  • Step-up SIP support — can you increase your SIP annually by a percentage?
  • Charts and visualisation — doughnut, bar, or line charts showing growth
  • Year-by-year breakdown — table showing invested vs returns each year
  • Real-time calculation — results update as you type (no "Calculate" button)
  • Mobile-friendly — works well on phone screens
  • No login required — can you use it without creating an account?

The 7 Best SIP Calculators

#1
ITR Stats SIP Calculator
itrstats.in/calculator/sip
Top Pick

A fast, accurate SIP calculator that updates results in real-time as you type. Supports both regular and step-up SIP with a dedicated annual increase slider. Shows an interactive doughnut chart (invested vs returns split) and a stacked bar chart with year-by-year growth. Includes a toggleable breakdown table with exact figures for each year.

Strengths

  • Real-time results — no "Calculate" button
  • Step-up SIP with annual increase %
  • Interactive doughnut + stacked bar charts
  • Year-by-year breakdown table
  • Correct monthly compounding formula
  • Dark mode, fast, zero ads
  • No login or account needed

Limitations

  • No direct link to start SIP in a fund
  • No XIRR or fund-specific return data
  • No PDF export of results
Best for: Anyone who wants instant, accurate SIP projections with step-up support and interactive charts — without signing up for anything.
#2
Groww SIP Calculator
groww.in/calculators/sip-calculator
Popular

Groww's SIP calculator is one of the most used in India thanks to their massive mutual fund platform. It features a clean slider-based UI with SIP and Lumpsum tabs. Uses the correct compound interest formula and clearly shows invested amount vs estimated returns in a pie chart. Step-up SIP is available as a separate calculator.

Strengths

  • Trusted by millions of Groww users
  • Clean, minimal interface
  • Correct compounding formula
  • SIP + Lumpsum toggle on same page
  • "Invest Now" button links to actual funds

Limitations

  • Step-up SIP is a separate calculator page
  • No year-by-year breakdown table
  • No bar chart for growth over time
  • Results update only on slider change (not typing)
Best for: Groww users who want a quick SIP estimate and the ability to start investing in the same platform.
#3
ClearTax SIP Calculator
cleartax.in/s/sip-calculator
Popular

ClearTax's calculator is embedded in their comprehensive tax and investment platform. It supports basic SIP calculation with a slider UI and shows a simple donut chart. The real value is its integration with ClearTax's ELSS investment flow — you can go from estimating returns to investing in a tax-saving fund in two clicks.

Strengths

  • Integrated with ELSS tax-saving investment
  • Clean slider interface
  • Shows invested vs returns pie chart
  • Well-known and trusted brand

Limitations

  • No step-up SIP option
  • No year-by-year growth table
  • Page is heavy with ads and ELSS promotions
  • No bar/line chart for growth over time
Best for: People planning ELSS tax-saving SIPs who want calculation + investment in the same workflow.
#4
ET Money SIP Calculator
etmoney.com

ET Money's SIP calculator stands out for its goal-based approach. Beyond the standard calculation, it recommends specific mutual funds based on your return expectations and offers a "SIP Step Up" feature. The UI uses sliders with a visual breakdown of invested vs returns.

Strengths

  • Goal-based investment recommendations
  • Step-up SIP support
  • Fund recommendations based on return target
  • Visually appealing slider UI

Limitations

  • Pushes premium app features heavily
  • No year-by-year breakdown table
  • Requires scrolling past marketing content
Best for: Goal-oriented investors who want SIP calculation with fund recommendations in one place.
#5
Angel One SIP Calculator
angelone.in/calculators/sip-calculator

Angel One (formerly Angel Broking) offers a functional SIP calculator as part of their trading platform. It provides the essentials — monthly amount, expected return, and tenure — with a bar chart showing the growth of your investment over time. Accurate formula implementation.

Strengths

  • Accurate calculations
  • Bar chart showing growth over time
  • Links to Angel One trading platform

Limitations

  • No step-up SIP
  • No year-by-year breakdown
  • Basic UI with limited customization
  • Heavy page with platform marketing
Best for: Angel One users who want a quick SIP estimate within their brokerage platform.
#6
Scripbox SIP Calculator
scripbox.com/calculators/sip-calculator

Scripbox positions itself as a wealth management platform, and their SIP calculator reflects this with a clean, minimalist design. It shows a simple breakdown of invested amount vs returns with a visual graph. Scripbox recommends their own curated fund portfolios alongside the results.

Strengths

  • Very clean, minimal design
  • Curated fund recommendations
  • Mobile-friendly interface

Limitations

  • No step-up SIP
  • No detailed breakdown table
  • Limited chart options
  • Pushes Scripbox portfolio products
Best for: New investors looking for a simple calculator paired with curated fund recommendations.
#7
BankBazaar SIP Calculator
bankbazaar.com

BankBazaar's SIP calculator is a straightforward tool that gets the basics right. Enter monthly investment, tenure, and expected return — it shows the maturity amount. No frills, no charts, just the numbers you need for a quick estimate.

Strengths

  • Simple and fast
  • No login required
  • Accurate formula

Limitations

  • No step-up SIP
  • No charts or visual breakdown
  • No year-by-year growth table
  • Ad-heavy page layout
Best for: Quick ballpark SIP estimates when you just need a number fast.
