7 Best EMI Calculators Online for 2026

We tested every major EMI calculator for formula accuracy, amortization schedules, and prepayment modelling. Here's which ones loan borrowers should actually trust.

February 26, 2026 9 min read Tool Comparison

Whether you're taking a home loan, car loan, or personal loan — the first thing you do is check the EMI. A ₹50 lakh home loan at 8.5% for 20 years means paying ₹43,391 every month and ₹54.14 lakh in total interest. That's more interest than the principal itself.

But here's what most EMI calculators don't show you: how much of each EMI goes toward principal vs interest, how prepayment changes your total cost, or what the full amortization schedule looks like. We tested 7 popular EMI calculators to find which ones go beyond the basics.

Quick Verdict

  • Best overall: ITR Stats — real-time results, amortization schedule, principal vs interest charts, interest-to-principal ratio
  • Best for prepayment: BankBazaar — model prepayment scenarios with interest savings
  • Best bank-specific: HDFC/ICICI — exact rates and eligibility for their loan products
  • Best for loan comparison: Bajaj Finserv — compare EMIs across loan types

What We Tested

For each calculator, we checked:

  • Formula accuracy — uses reducing balance method: EMI = P × r × (1+r)^n / [(1+r)^n - 1]
  • Amortization schedule — month-by-month or year-by-year principal vs interest breakdown
  • Prepayment modelling — can you add lump-sum prepayments and see interest savings?
  • Charts — visual split of principal vs interest, payment timeline
  • Loan type presets — home, car, personal, education with typical rate ranges
  • Tax benefit info — mentions Section 80C (principal) and 24(b) (interest) for home loans
  • Real-time calculation — updates as you type

The 7 Best EMI Calculators

#1
ITR Stats EMI Calculator
itrstats.in/calculator/emi
Top Pick

A comprehensive EMI calculator that shows monthly EMI, total interest, total payment, and interest-to-principal ratio — all updating in real-time. Features an interactive doughnut chart (principal vs interest split), stacked bar chart (year-by-year payment breakdown), and a toggleable amortization schedule showing exact principal and interest components for each year.

Strengths

  • Real-time results — no button
  • Full amortization schedule
  • Interactive doughnut + bar charts
  • Interest as % of principal shown
  • Year-by-year principal vs interest split
  • Correct reducing balance formula
  • Dark mode, no ads, no login

Limitations

  • No prepayment modelling
  • No loan type presets
  • No processing fee calculation
Best for: Anyone who wants a clear visual understanding of their loan structure with an amortization schedule, without signing up for anything.
#2
BankBazaar EMI Calculator
bankbazaar.com/emi-calculator
Popular

BankBazaar's EMI calculator is the most popular in India for loan calculations. It supports multiple loan types (home, car, personal, education), shows a detailed amortization schedule, and uniquely offers prepayment modelling — add a lump-sum payment and see how much interest you save. Also shows live loan rates from multiple banks.

Strengths

  • Prepayment modelling with interest savings
  • Multiple loan type tabs
  • Amortization schedule included
  • Live bank loan rates
  • Very high brand recognition

Limitations

  • Extremely ad-heavy page
  • No real-time calculation (button click)
  • Lead generation forms pop up frequently
  • Slow page load
Best for: People planning prepayment strategy who want to see exactly how much interest they'll save.
#3
HDFC Bank EMI Calculator
hdfcbank.com
Bank Tool

HDFC Bank's EMI calculator uses their current lending rates automatically. It shows EMI, total interest, and total payment for HDFC-specific loan products. Useful for existing HDFC customers or those applying for an HDFC loan, as it reflects the exact rate you'll get.

Strengths

  • Uses exact HDFC lending rates
  • Separate home, car, personal loan tools
  • Shows processing fee estimates
  • Links to loan application

Limitations

  • HDFC rates only — no comparison
  • No amortization schedule
  • No prepayment modelling
  • Heavy website, slow to navigate
Best for: HDFC customers checking their exact EMI before applying for a loan.
#4
Groww EMI Calculator
groww.in/calculators/emi-calculator
Popular

Groww's EMI calculator follows their standard clean design. Slider-based inputs for amount, rate, and tenure with a pie chart showing principal vs interest split. The surrounding content explains reducing balance vs flat rate and includes tips on choosing loan tenure.

Strengths

  • Clean, ad-light interface
  • Pie chart for visual split
  • Reducing vs flat rate explained
  • Accurate formula

Limitations

  • No amortization schedule
  • No prepayment modelling
  • Slider-only input
  • No year-by-year breakdown
Best for: Quick EMI estimate with a clean interface and no distractions.
#5
ICICI Bank EMI Calculator
icicibank.com
Bank Tool

ICICI Bank offers separate EMI calculators for home loan, car loan, and personal loan — each pre-loaded with ICICI's current rates. Shows year-by-year amortization table with reducing principal balance. Ties directly into ICICI's loan application process.

Strengths

  • Separate calculators by loan type
  • Amortization table included
  • Current ICICI rates pre-loaded

Limitations

  • ICICI rates only
  • No prepayment option
  • No charts or visual breakdown
  • Cluttered banking website
Best for: ICICI customers who want an amortization schedule with their bank's actual rates.
#6
Bajaj Finserv EMI Calculator
bajajfinserv.in/emi-calculator

Bajaj Finserv's calculator covers a wide range of loan products — home, personal, business, two-wheeler, and more. Each loan type has typical rate ranges pre-filled. Shows EMI, total interest, and total payment with a visual chart. Ties into their loan application and pre-approved offer checking.

Strengths

  • Wide range of loan types
  • Pre-approved offer checking
  • Visual payment chart
  • Typical rate ranges pre-filled

Limitations

  • No amortization schedule
  • Lead generation focused
  • No prepayment modelling
  • Heavy marketing content
Best for: Bajaj Finserv customers checking EMI across multiple loan products.
#7
ClearTax EMI Calculator
cleartax.in/s/emi-calculator

ClearTax's EMI calculator is straightforward — enter amount, rate, tenure, get EMI. The real value is their detailed content on home loan tax benefits under Section 80C and 24(b), which helps borrowers understand the tax implications alongside the EMI calculation.

Strengths

  • Home loan tax benefit guide
  • Section 80C and 24(b) explained
  • Trusted tax platform

Limitations

  • Basic calculator features
  • No amortization schedule
  • No prepayment or charts
  • Ad-heavy page
Best for: Home loan borrowers who want to understand the tax benefits alongside their EMI.
