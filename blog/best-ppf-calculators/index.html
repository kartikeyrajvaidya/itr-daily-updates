7 Best PPF Calculators Online for 2026

We tested every popular PPF calculator for accuracy, yearly breakdown, and tax info. Here's which ones get the maturity value right.

February 26, 2026 8 min read Tool Comparison

PPF (Public Provident Fund) is one of India's most popular long-term savings instruments — and for good reason. It offers guaranteed returns at 7.1% with EEE tax status, meaning your investment, interest, and maturity are all completely tax-free.

But when you Google "PPF calculator," you get dozens of results — and they don't all work the same way. Some use the wrong formula, some don't show yearly breakdowns, and most give you just one number with no visual context of how your money grows over 15+ years.

We tested 7 PPF calculators with identical inputs — ₹1,50,000 yearly deposit, 7.1% rate, 15 years — and compared their results. The correct maturity value is ₹40,68,209.

Quick Verdict

  • Best overall: ITR Stats — real-time results, interactive charts, year-by-year table, EEE tax badge, dark mode
  • Best for bank-specific info: Groww — links to SBI, HDFC, Post Office PPF account pages
  • Best for tax planning: ClearTax — PPF calculator integrated with 80C deduction planning
  • Quickest estimate: BankBazaar — simple, fast, no-frills

What We Tested

For each calculator, we checked:

  • Formula accuracy — uses correct PPF formula: FV = P × (1+r) × [(1+r)^n - 1] / r
  • Current rate (7.1%) — defaults to or supports the current PPF interest rate
  • Year-by-year breakdown — table showing opening balance, deposit, interest, closing balance each year
  • Charts and visualization — doughnut/bar charts showing invested vs interest earned
  • Tax information — mentions EEE status, Section 80C eligibility
  • Extension beyond 15 years — allows modelling tenure beyond the mandatory lock-in
  • Real-time calculation — updates as you type
