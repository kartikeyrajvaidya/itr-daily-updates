7 Best FD Calculators Online for 2026

We tested every major fixed deposit calculator for compounding accuracy, TDS info, and effective yield display. Here's which ones actually get it right.

February 26, 2026 8 min read Tool Comparison

Fixed deposits remain India's most trusted savings instrument — over ₹100 lakh crore is parked in bank FDs. But comparing FD returns across banks is harder than it should be because compounding frequency, TDS, and effective yield all affect the final maturity amount.

We tested 7 popular FD calculators with the same inputs — ₹5,00,000 at 7% for 5 years with quarterly compounding. The correct maturity amount is ₹7,09,259 with an effective annual yield of 7.19%.

Quick Verdict

  • Best overall: ITR Stats — 4 compounding frequencies, effective yield, TDS info, charts, year-by-year table, real-time
  • Best for rate comparison: BankBazaar — shows live FD rates from 30+ banks
  • Best bank-specific: HDFC/SBI — use the bank's own calculator for exact rates and special tenures
  • Best for tax planning: ClearTax — FD interest taxation guidance included

What We Tested

For each calculator, we checked:

  • Compounding frequency options — monthly, quarterly, half-yearly, annually
  • Effective annual yield — shows the real rate after compounding
  • TDS information — mentions ₹40K/₹50K threshold, 10% TDS, Form 15G/H
  • Charts and breakdown — visual split of principal vs interest, year-by-year table
  • Real-time calculation — updates as you type
  • Senior citizen rates — mentions the extra 0.25-0.5% for 60+ depositors

The 7 Best FD Calculators

#1
ITR Stats FD Calculator
itrstats.in/calculator/fd
Top Pick

The most complete FD calculator we tested. Choose from 4 compounding frequencies (monthly, quarterly, half-yearly, annually) and see how each affects your maturity amount. Shows effective annual yield, TDS information with the ₹40K threshold, interactive doughnut chart, stacked bar chart, and a toggleable year-by-year breakdown. All results update in real-time.

Strengths

  • 4 compounding frequencies
  • Effective annual yield displayed
  • TDS info with threshold amounts
  • Real-time results — no button needed
  • Interactive doughnut + bar charts
  • Year-by-year breakdown table
  • Dark mode, no ads, no login

Limitations

  • No live bank FD rates
  • No senior citizen rate toggle
  • No cumulative vs non-cumulative FD option
Best for: Anyone who wants to understand exactly how compounding frequency affects FD returns, with visual breakdowns and tax info.
#2
Groww FD Calculator
groww.in/calculators/fd-calculator
Popular

Groww's FD calculator is clean and straightforward. It uses a slider-based UI with fields for deposit amount, interest rate, and tenure. Shows a pie chart of principal vs interest earned. Groww also lists current FD rates from major banks alongside the calculator, which is useful for comparison shopping.

Strengths

  • Clean, ad-light interface
  • Current bank FD rates listed
  • Pie chart for visual breakdown
  • Accurate calculations

Limitations

  • No compounding frequency selector
  • No effective yield display
  • No year-by-year breakdown
  • Assumes quarterly compounding only
Best for: Quick FD estimates alongside current bank rates for comparison.
#3
BankBazaar FD Calculator
bankbazaar.com/fixed-deposit-calculator
Popular

BankBazaar's FD calculator stands out for its live rate comparison feature. It shows current FD rates from 30+ banks side by side, making it easy to find the best rate. The calculator itself is basic but the rate comparison table is the best in the market.

Strengths

  • Live FD rates from 30+ banks
  • Rate comparison table
  • Special tenure rates listed
  • Senior citizen rates shown

Limitations

  • No compounding frequency option
  • No effective yield
  • Very ad-heavy page
  • No charts or visual breakdown
Best for: Comparing FD rates across banks to find the best deal before depositing.
#4
HDFC Bank FD Calculator
hdfcbank.com
Bank Tool

HDFC Bank's own calculator uses their current FD rates automatically — you just enter the amount and tenure. It shows the exact maturity amount you'd get at HDFC. Useful for existing HDFC customers or anyone considering an HDFC FD, but only calculates for HDFC rates.

Strengths

  • Uses exact HDFC FD rates
  • Auto-selects rate based on tenure
  • Shows senior citizen rates

Limitations

  • HDFC rates only — no comparison
  • No compounding frequency choice
  • No visual charts or breakdown
  • Requires navigating HDFC's heavy website
Best for: HDFC customers who want the exact maturity amount for their deposit.
#5
ClearTax FD Calculator
cleartax.in/s/fd-calculator

ClearTax's FD calculator is basic but includes useful content on FD taxation — TDS rules, Form 15G/H, and how FD interest is taxed at slab rates. The calculator is tied to their broader tax planning platform, making it useful for understanding the post-tax impact of FD investments.

Strengths

  • Comprehensive FD tax guide
  • Form 15G/H information
  • TDS rules explained
  • Trusted platform

Limitations

  • Basic calculator features
  • No compounding options
  • No charts or yearly breakdown
  • Ad-heavy page
Best for: Understanding how FD interest is taxed and planning TDS accordingly.
#6
SBI FD Calculator
sbi.co.in
Bank Tool

SBI's website shows their current FD rates by tenure but the calculator functionality is limited. You can check rates for different tenures and calculate manually using their rate card. Best used to verify SBI-specific rates before opening a deposit.

Strengths

  • Official SBI rates — always current
  • Senior citizen rates shown
  • Special tenure rates listed

Limitations

  • Rate card only — limited calculator
  • Dated website interface
  • No visual breakdown or charts
  • No real calculator functionality
Best for: SBI customers verifying current deposit rates for specific tenures.
#7
Scripbox FD Calculator
scripbox.com/calculators/fd-calculator

Scripbox offers a minimalist FD calculator with a clean design. It shows principal, interest earned, and maturity value. The surrounding content compares FD with mutual fund alternatives, which is clearly designed to nudge users toward Scripbox's own investment products.

Strengths

  • Clean, minimal design
  • FD vs MF comparison content
  • Accurate calculations

Limitations

  • No compounding options
  • No effective yield or TDS info
  • Pushes MF alternatives
  • No charts or breakdown
Best for: People comparing FD returns with mutual fund alternatives.
