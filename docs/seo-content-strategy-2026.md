# SEO Content Strategy & Keyword Research Plan (ITR Stats)

**Prepared:** February 11, 2026  
**Objective:** Make `itrstats.in` the go-to website for ITR stats and tax-related info across both filing and non-filing seasons.

## Research Summary

### High-demand keyword patterns
- Head terms are dominated by navigational intent:
  - `income tax` (~6.12M)
  - `income tax login` (~5.0M)
  - `itr login` (~2.74M)
  - `e filing` (~1.83M)
  - `efiling` (~1.5M)
- Strong evergreen opportunity:
  - `tax calculator` (~1.22M)
- Mid-volume, high-intent support queries:
  - `traces login` (~673K)
  - `ais password` (~37K)
  - `80ddb deduction` (~19K)
  - `capital gain tax` (~7.8K)

### What this means for ITR Stats
- Competing directly on pure navigation queries is hard (official + large incumbent portals dominate).
- Best path: own the **processing intelligence layer**:
  - What is happening right now in processing/refunds
  - What it means for taxpayers
  - What actions users should take next

## Strategic Positioning

Position ITR Stats as:
- **Live Tax Processing Intelligence Platform** (daily + weekly stats interpretation)
- **Practical Tax Operations Guide** (deadlines, mismatch fixes, compliance workflows)
- **Decision-support utility hub** (calculators + explainers)

## 90-Day Publishing Plan

### Weeks 1-3
- Publish pillar: **ITR Refund Tracker India (Live)**
- Publish pillar: **ITR Processing Time Dashboard** (7-day / 30-day velocity + backlog burn-down)
- Publish guide: **143(1) Intimation Decoder**

### Weeks 4-6
- Publish: **Tax Due Dates 2026** (month-wise hub + countdown blocks)
- Publish: **E-Verification in 30 Days** (action checklist)
- Publish: **AIS/26AS/TIS Reconciliation** (mismatch handling)

### Weeks 7-12
- Publish: **ITR-U Explained** (what can/cannot be fixed)
- Continue **Weekly Processing Update** on the same URL (freshness signal)
- Publish: **Belated vs Revised vs Updated Return**
- Publish: **Refund Delay Troubleshooter**

## 12-Month Editorial Rhythm

- **Jan-Mar:** refund backlog and processing diagnostics
- **Apr-Jun:** new rules, form prep, AIS/26AS checks, Form 16 readiness
- **Jul-Sep:** filing-season war room content, high-frequency updates
- **Oct-Dec:** belated/revised return guidance, notices, refund follow-through

## Site Architecture Recommendations

- Add `/tax-stats/` for live and trend pages
- Add `/tax-guides/` for evergreen explanatory content
- Keep `/blog/weekly-update.html` as a recurring freshness URL
- Every guide should internally link to:
  - one live stats page
  - one calculator page

## KPI Targets (Next 6 Months)

- Rank top-10 for at least 15 non-brand tax-info keywords
- 3x off-season organic traffic (Oct-Mar baseline comparison)
- 25%+ returning-user share on tracker pages
- 5%+ CTR on update pages during filing season
