## Voice & Tone
- **Short.** If it needs more than 5 lines to make the point, cut it.
- **Numbers first.** Lead with the stat, then explain.
- **No jargon.** Write like you're texting a friend who asks "bro how much tax will I pay on 12 lakh?"
- **Confident, not preachy.** Share data. Let people draw conclusions.
