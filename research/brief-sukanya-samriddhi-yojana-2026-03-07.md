## Step 10 - Key Facts Verified

- Interest rate: **8.2% p.a.** compounded annually, for Q1-Q4 FY 2025-26
- Min deposit: **Rs 250/year**
- Max deposit: **Rs 1.5 lakh/year**
- Contribution period: **15 years** from opening
- Maturity: **21 years** from opening
- Partial withdrawal: **50%** after girl turns 18 or passes 10th standard
- EEE status: contributions (80C), interest (Sec 10), maturity - all exempt
- New regime: 80C deduction NOT available; interest + maturity still tax-free
- Accounts opened: **4.53 crore** (PIB, Jan 2026)
- Total deposits: **Rs 3.33 lakh crore** (PIB, Dec 2025)
- Maturity calculation (Rs 1.5L/yr, 15 yrs, 8.2%, 21-yr horizon): ~Rs 69 lakh
