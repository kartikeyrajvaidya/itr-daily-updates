## The Opportunity in One Line

The "how rare is my salary in India" query space is completely unowned — the only data-driven article that existed (thememo.in substack) is now a parked domain. ITR Stats has the data, the calculator, and the CBDT citation to own this cluster permanently.

## Data Sources Confirmed Available

| Source | What It Has | Year | Accessibility |
|--------|-------------|------|---------------|
| CBDT ITR Statistics | Income slab counts (exact filer counts at each bracket), state-wise filing totals | AY 2023-24 (latest) | Free PDF at incometaxindia.gov.in |
| PLFS 2023-24 | Average wages by employment type (salaried ₹21,103/mo), state-wise employment data | 2023-24 | Free download dge.gov.in |
| WID 2024 (Piketty team) | Top 1% income share = 22.6% of national income; top 1% wealth share = 40.1% | 2022-23 | Free PDF wid.world |
| UBS Global Wealth Report 2025 | India median wealth per adult, millionaire count, wealth Gini | 2024 | Free PDF ubs.com |
| SBI Research 2023 | Gini fell 0.472→0.402 (2014-23), ITR filers at ₹5-10L grew 295% | 2023 | PIB press release |
| AIDIS NSS 77th round | Household asset/liability data | 2019 | Free mospi.gov.in (DATED) |

**Key CBDT numbers for all articles:**
- Total ITR filers: ~8.18 crore (AY 2023-24)
- Above ₹50 lakh: 9.39 lakh filers = top 1.1% of filers
- Above ₹1 crore: 2.16–3.24 lakh filers = top 0.26-0.4% of filers
- Above ₹10 lakh: ~69 lakh filers = top ~8% of filers, top ~2% of working population
- PLFS: Only top 22% of workers earn more than ₹15,000/month
- PLFS: Bottom 27.5% earn less than ₹3,000/month

## SEO Notes Across All Articles

- **Schema:** FAQPage + Article on every page. Dataset schema on data hub pages.
- **Featured snippet target:** Yes on all — lead with direct answer in first 100 words.
- **AI Overview risk:** Medium. Mitigate by using numbers not available in AI training (latest CBDT AY 2023-24 data, ITR Stats live calculator).
- **Freshness signal:** Add "Updated: [Month Year]" to every article. CBDT data updates annually (typically Q3-Q4 each year). Plan annual refresh.
- **Images:** Chart.js income distribution chart (already used on income-distribution page) should be embedded in all salary articles.
- **Meta description formula:** Lead with the number. "₹10 lakh/year = top 8% of India's ITR filers. See how rare your salary is among 8.18 crore taxpayers — based on official CBDT data."
