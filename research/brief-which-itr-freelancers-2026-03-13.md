## Intent & differentiation

- **Primary query:** "which ITR form for freelancers" / "which ITR for self employed"
- **Answer:** Almost always **ITR-3** or **ITR-4 (Sugam)**. ITR-4 = presumptive (44AD, 44ADA, 44AE); ITR-3 = with books or ineligible for presumptive.
- **No cannibalization:** Salaried post = ITR-1 vs ITR-2. This = ITR-3 vs ITR-4. itr-form-comparison covers all forms; this is freelancer-first narrative.
- **Data hook:** ITR-4 = 2.13 Cr (27.4%), ITR-3 = 1.25 Cr (16.1%) from itr-form-comparison. "2.13 crore file ITR-4 (Sugam); freelancers and small business dominate."
